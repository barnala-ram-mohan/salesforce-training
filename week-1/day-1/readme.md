1. What is CRM?
CRM refers to Customer Relationship Management. It is a system used by companies to manage customer information, interactions, and sales in one place.
2. Why companies use Salesforce?
Companies use Salesforce to manage customers, track sales, automate tasks, and improve business efficiency.
3. Explain *Account *Contact *Oppurtunity ?
   Account: A company or organization.
   Contact: A person working in that company.
   Opportunity: A possible sales deal with the company.
4.Real-world mapping:
   Account = Company (TCS)
   Contact = Person (Manager in TCS)
   Opportunity = Deal (Selling product/service to TCS)
